April 20 Update: Automated Student Account Creation via LMS
Hello LMS Administrator,
Beginning April 20, 2026, Pearson will enable Automated User Onboarding (AUO) for students in LMS‑integrated courses.
With this enhancement, student Pearson accounts will be automatically created and linked when students launch Pearson content from the LMS—using the first name, last name, and email address already provided by your institution. This removes the need for manual account creation and allows students to access course materials more quickly, with fewer clicks and less account management.
Why this is beneficial:
  • Faster, first‑day access to Pearson materials
  • Reduced student friction and support issues
  • Improved data consistency using institution‑provided information
Opt‑out option:
If you prefer not to enable this feature, you may opt out on or after April 20 by contacting integrationstpm@pearson.com.
